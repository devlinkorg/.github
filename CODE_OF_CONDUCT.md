# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

DevLink is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Treat all participants with respect and professionalism.
- Provide and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community.
- Show empathy and consideration in all interactions.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Trolling, derogatory comments, or inflammatory language.
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all DevLink spaces, including:

- Repository discussions, issues, and pull requests
- Community channels and forums
- Events (online and in-person)
- Any space where an individual represents DevLink

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the DevLink moderation team at **conduct@devlink.org**.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and confidentially. The moderation team is obligated to maintain the privacy of the reporter.

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

1. **Warning** — A private written notice clarifying the violation and expected behavior.
2. **Temporary suspension** — Removal from community participation for a defined period.
3. **Permanent ban** — Permanent removal from all DevLink spaces.

The severity of the response will be proportional to the violation.
