# Community

## About

The DevLink community is composed of engineers, architects, and technical contributors who collaborate on building production-grade systems. Participation is open to anyone who aligns with our [Code of Conduct](../CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) and is willing to contribute meaningfully.

---

## Communication Channels

| Channel | Purpose | Access |
|---|---|---|
| GitHub Discussions | Technical discussions, proposals, Q&A | Public |
| Guild Channels | Domain-specific coordination | Guild members |
| Project Channels | Project-level communication | Project team |

---

## Roles

### Contributor

Any individual who has submitted at least one accepted contribution (code, documentation, review, or issue report).

### Guild Member

A contributor who has been accepted into a specific guild based on demonstrated expertise and sustained participation.

### Guild Lead

Responsible for the direction, standards, and coordination of a guild. Guild leads are selected based on technical depth and leadership within the domain.

### Maintainer

Individuals with merge access to one or more repositories. Maintainers are responsible for code quality, review standards, and release management.

### Core Team

The organizational leadership responsible for cross-guild coordination, strategic direction, and governance.

---

## Expectations

All community members are expected to:

- Communicate respectfully and professionally.
- Contribute constructively to discussions and reviews.
- Follow established workflows and standards.
- Support and mentor newer contributors when possible.

---

## Recognition

DevLink recognizes meaningful contributions through:

- **Contributor listings** in project and guild documentation.
- **Role progression** based on sustained, quality contributions.
- **Project ownership** opportunities for proven contributors.
