# Events and Initiatives

## Overview

DevLink organizes recurring events and initiatives to foster collaboration, knowledge sharing, and continuous improvement across the engineering community.

---

## Recurring Events

### Build Sprints

**Frequency:** Bi-weekly
**Format:** Focused 2-week development cycles where teams collaborate on specific project milestones.

- Each sprint has defined objectives and deliverables.
- Sprint retrospectives are conducted at the end of each cycle.
- Progress is tracked through GitHub Projects.

### Technical Reviews

**Frequency:** Weekly
**Format:** Peer-led sessions where teams present architectural decisions, implementation approaches, or system designs for feedback.

- Open to all contributors.
- Presentations follow a structured format: problem, approach, tradeoffs, decision.
- Notes are published in the relevant project or guild documentation.

### Knowledge Exchange

**Frequency:** Monthly
**Format:** Deep-dive sessions on specific technologies, patterns, or lessons learned from active projects.

- Topics are proposed by community members.
- Sessions are recorded and archived for future reference.
- Follow-up resources are shared in guild channels.

---

## Initiatives

### Onboarding Cohorts

Structured onboarding programs for new contributors. Each cohort is guided by experienced members through the contribution workflow, codebase orientation, and first-issue completion.

### Documentation Drives

Periodic campaigns to improve, expand, and standardize documentation across all projects and guilds.

### Cross-Guild Collaborations

Time-boxed initiatives where members from different guilds collaborate on projects that span multiple domains (e.g., deploying an ML model with a web interface and automated infrastructure).

---

## Proposing an Event

To propose a new event or initiative:

1. Open a [Discussion](https://github.com/devlinkorg/.github/discussions) with the `event-proposal` label.
2. Include: objective, format, frequency, and required resources.
3. The core team will review and respond within one week.

---

## Calendar

A shared event calendar is maintained in the community channels. All scheduled events include agendas and preparation materials posted at least 48 hours in advance.
